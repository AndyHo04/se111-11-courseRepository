BIO-ABOUT ME

My name is Andy Ho and I am 19 years old. I am a Vietnamese-American as my parents came over from Vietnam and gave birth to me in Rhode Island. I grew up in Woonsocket and moved to Warwick during fourth grade of Primary School. I graduated from Pilgrim High School during the summer of 2023 and I started to attend New England Institute of Technology in the Fall of 2023. I am currently a software enginner major because computers have always been interesting and I wanted to do something related to engineering and computers. I am a very quiet and introverted person who has been trying to break out of my shell and do things that I would have never done when I was younger.
